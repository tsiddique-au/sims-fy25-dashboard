Executive
Summary

FY25 marked a decisive financial turnaround for Sims Limited. Group Underlying EBITDA surged +48.1% to $430.0M, driven by North America Metal margin expansion (+67.5% EBITDA) and a record SA Recycling JV contribution of $120.0M. Underlying EBIT recovered +198% to $174.9M and NPAT returned to profitability at $83.1M. These gains partially offset a structural headwind in ANZ, where Chinese steel export pressure compressed trading margins 190bps and EBITDA fell −7.9%. Sims Lifecycle Services accelerated strongly (+22% revenue, +84% EBIT), positioning for the AI-driven data centre cycle. Net debt fell 19.3% to $332.3M; free cash flow turned positive at $106.8M. Full-year dividend increased +130% to 23.0¢ per share.
